## Option A: Industrial Precision — Brutalist-Minimal Engineering

**Design Movement:** Industrial Brutalism meets Swiss Grid Minimalism

**Core Principles:**
1. Raw structural honesty — exposed grid lines, stark contrast, no decorative fluff
2. Typography as architecture — letterforms carry structural weight
3. Navy + white + amber as the only palette — no gradients, no softness
4. Every element earns its place — zero decoration without function

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Pure white (#FFFFFF) and deep navy (#0D1B2A)
- Accent: Amber/gold (#C8922A) — engineering authority and warmth
- Text: Charcoal (#1A1A2E) on white, white on navy
- Emotional intent: Trustworthy, precise, no-nonsense

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric split layouts — left-heavy text blocks with right-side imagery
- Full-bleed hero with dark overlay, left-aligned headline
- Section headers use spaced uppercase tracking (like ZEM reference)
- Services in a 2-column grid with left border accent lines

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin horizontal rule lines separating sections (1px navy)
2. Amber accent bar on left of section headings
3. Uppercase spaced eyebrow labels before main headings

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states: subtle underline slide-in on links, border color shift on cards
- CTA buttons: solid navy fill with amber hover, sharp corners

**Animation:**
- Entrance: fade-up on scroll for section content (subtle, 300ms)
- No parallax, no complex motion — engineering precision

**Typography System:**
- Display: Barlow Condensed Bold — industrial, structural
- Body: Source Sans 3 Regular — highly readable, professional
- Eyebrow labels: Barlow SemiBold, tracked uppercase
</text>

## Option C: Clean Authority — Professional Engineering Firm (CHOSEN)

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Professional Services / Refined Minimalism

**Core Principles:**
1. White-dominant with strategic navy depth — clean, authoritative, approachable
2. Amber/gold accent for warmth and differentiation (matching ZEM reference style)
3. Asymmetric section layouts — left-aligned text with right-side visual weight
4. Generous whitespace as a signal of confidence and clarity

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Off-white (#F8F7F4) and pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Primary: Deep navy (#0D2240) — authority and trust
- Accent: Warm amber (#C8922A) — engineering warmth, Bay Area warmth
- Text: Dark charcoal (#1C2B3A) for body, navy for headings
- Emotional intent: Trustworthy, modern, approachable, Bay Area professional

- Full-bleed aerial construction photo hero, dark overlay, left-aligned headline
- Sticky nav: logo left, links center-right, CTA button rightmost
